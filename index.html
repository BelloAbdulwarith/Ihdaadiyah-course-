Course Overview

The Arabic and Islamic Studies Crash Course for Ihdaadiyah Level is designed for students ready to refine their foundational knowledge. Dive into advanced grammar, syntax, Qur'anic studies, and Islamic sciences in this intensive program.

Key Features

Refine grammar, reading, and writing skills through engaging exercises and real-world applications.

Explore Tafseer, Hadith, and Fiqh with a focus on connecting classical knowledge to modern life.

Master the art of Qur'anic recitation with precise pronunciation and memorization techniques.

Learn about key historical events and the contributions of Islamic scholars throughout history.

Course Duration

This intensive program spans 1 year with 4 sessions per week, each lasting 1 hour. Flexible scheduling is available for online and in-person learning.

Who Is This Course For?

Designed for students with a foundational understanding of Arabic and Islamic studies, this course is perfect for those looking to prepare for advanced learning and build confidence in their skills.
